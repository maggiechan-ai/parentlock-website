Questions

You're probably wondering…

Can I really not unlock my phone during a session?

That's the whole point. Once you start a session, your selected apps are locked until the timer ends. Emergency calls always work. You chose the duration — we just hold you to it. Think of it as a commitment device, not a punishment.

What age range is this for?

ParentLock is designed for parents of children aged 0–5. Our prompts are tailored to six developmental stages: 0–6 months, 6–12 months, 1–2 years, 2–3 years, 3–4 years, and 4–5 years. Each prompt is written specifically for what your child is learning right now.

What if I have multiple children?

Add as many children as you like. Each gets their own profile with age-appropriate prompts. You can start a session for one child or all of them at once.

Is this app anti-technology?

Not even slightly. We're pro-presence. Phones are incredible tools — they're just not great at sharing your attention with a toddler. ParentLock helps you choose when to be on and when to be present.

What about my privacy?

All your data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or sell any personal information. ParentLock is built to support families, not monetise them.

Why isn't there a monthly or annual plan?

Simple: $4.99/week is easy to try and easy to cancel. No big commitment. If it works for your family, it's worth it. If it doesn't, cancel anytime. No lock-in. (Ironic, we know.)
